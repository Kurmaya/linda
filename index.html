About

I’m Linda Keller - a bold, funny, speaker here to wake people up to how amazing God really is! My talks connect science, health, and Scripture—showing how what we’re now proving in brain science has been in the Bible all along.

I help women discover that faith isn’t boring or stuffy—it’s powerful, healing, and a lot cooler than they thought. I bring joyful energy, real-life “aha!” moments, and a call to live fully for the Lord.

Whether it’s a women’s retreat, church event, or ministry gathering, I speak with joyful expectation and a spark that lingers long after the event ends. Let’s laugh, learn, and fall in love with Jesus all over again.
